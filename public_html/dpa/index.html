Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

Last updated: November 30, 2025

1. Definitions

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the Terms of Service between you ("Customer") and Tasu ("Processor") concerning the processing of Personal Data.

2. Scope and Roles

Customer acts as the Data Controller and Tasu acts as the Data Processor. Tasu processes Personal Data only on behalf of and in accordance with Customer's documented instructions.

3. Data Processing

Tasu will process Personal Data only for the purpose of providing analytics services as described in the Terms of Service. Processing includes collection, storage, analysis, and deletion of click tracking data.

4. Security Measures

Tasu implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments.

5. Sub-processors

Tasu uses the following sub-processors: Tinybird (analytics data storage), Firebase (authentication), and Stripe (payment processing). Customer consents to the engagement of these sub-processors.

6. Data Subject Rights

Tasu will assist Customer in responding to requests from data subjects to exercise their rights under applicable data protection laws.

7. Data Deletion

Upon termination of services, Tasu will delete or return all Personal Data to Customer and delete existing copies unless storage is required by applicable law.
